Overview

This website contains notes and information for the module Food Process Design & Optimisation.

Lectures and Schedule

This module involves two lectures each week.

Lectures will be at 9 am Tuesday (CONN_S3) and 2 pm Thursday (FSB_322).

There are 6 laboratory practicals in this module, which will not start until 3 weeks into term. More detail will be provided before the labs start.

How You Will Be Assessed

Assessment for the module is as follows:

  • 15%: in-class exam
  • 15%: lab reports
  • 70%: formal exam

The content of the course has the following general structure:

  • Food Physics
  • Fluid Statics
  • Fluid Dynamics
  • Separation Science
  • Thermal Processing
  • Evaporation & Drying
  • Bulk Solids Science
  • Sustainable Processing
  • Topics in Engineering

Individual topics can take 2-4 lectures to complete, depending on class interest and progress.

Some topics may not be covered if the class needs additional time on a specific topic. Only topics covered in lectures will be examined.
